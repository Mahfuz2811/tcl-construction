Our MISSION & VISION

TRUST CONSORTIUM LTD. is determined to be one of the country’s leading company of civil work, Transmission line works, Power Plant works, manufacturer of all kinds of the metal tower like as Electrical Tower, Wireless Tower, Observation Tower and Tower making martial parts implements Telecommunication tower manufacturers, Erector and BTS installer. We aim to build ourselves as a world-class Power Plant and Transmission line contractor providing total customer satisfaction in all aspects of consumer and business needs. To achieve this, we have committed ourselves to maintain uncompromising product quality, competitive price, on-time product delivery, and customer support & maintenance services, and excellent teamwork.

  • To become a pre-eminent power plant works and civil work contractor, providing total customer care that the business & professional communities hold in high regard. We promise to provide customer care with expertise.
  • Our mission is to serve with sincerity and integrity. Our management believes in total customer satisfaction to ensure significant contribution to improve clients business performance in civil works – we shall strive to achieve “Marketplace Leadership” by building a business with best–in–class capacities to deliver measurable results for our clients.
  • The business value of TRUST CONSORTIUM LTD. serves as a guide to us in achieving our corporate vision and mission. It is this value that will help to shape ourselves into a world-class power plant, Transmission line, and Civil works. To attain our objectives, we have an action-oriented policy which is based on:-
  • Technology
  • Expertise
  • Commitment
  • Competitive
  • Quality
