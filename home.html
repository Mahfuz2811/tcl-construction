About Comppany

TRUST CONSORTIUM LTD is a registered company in Bangladesh. Mainly established for various kinds of civil works, 33Kv River Crossing Transmission line works, Power plant works, manufacturing every kind of metal towers like as Electrical Tower, Wireless Tower, Telephone Tower, Observation Tower and Tower making materials parts implements, providing construction management and earth filling, general contracting and supply construction material to government development work throughout Bangladesh and accessories by establishing one or more factory of modern Technology. With a team of young and energetic engineers and management experts, TRUST CONSORTIUM LTD reflects is are curring me of achievement and Rapid growth. Having affiliated with the most experienced companies which Major in Civil works, Transmission Line Works, Power Plant works, and structural fabrication facilities, and in the case of another are having high skilled civil engineering capabilities. Yes, the Trust consortium is providing a new dimension in the power plant works of value-added in Bangladesh.
